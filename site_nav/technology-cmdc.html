Centrally Managed Digital Currency - $CMDC
A digital currency and asset class, built on Gobble Chain private blockchain technology.


Centrally Managed Digital Currency is stylized as $CMDC.

A framework for $CMDC was developed in 2020. It is a prototype digital currency and asset class with a specification which pegs $CMDC to a defined existing currency to determine its value. Due to the way it was developed, it was inherently flawed. The original value rate was proposed to be 1:1.25. For example, 1 US dollar would equate to 1.25 $CMDC. However, big development changes to the mining process altered the original proposed value rate and it was adapted to 1:2500.

Bitcoin is infamous for being energy and resource intensive during the mining process. Some analysts speculated that its high rate of energy use could see its eventual demise. $CMDC has been designed specifically to be light on system resources for both mining and data storage. This helps increase energy efficiency and ease the data storage burden, while being able to mine large amounts. During the mining process, it may push all cores of the central processing unit(s) to maximum capacity, but memory usage remains moderate. While there is no technical limitation to the total number of $CMDC blocks that can be put into circulation, there are restrictions on the amount of available blocks in holding, in the social distribution vaults. This is a necessary component for maintaining centralized management of $CMDC and its assets. Currently, there is an imposed limit of 1 trillion $CMDC, per social distribution vault, spread across a maximum of 4 vaults.

$CMDC is mined using a parallel processing and file duplication combination, designed specifically to operate on Linux-based operating systems. Mined blocks are 'created' from a raw source block, called "block0". To prevent fraudulent duplication of blocks, each individual block is dated and timestamped, and cannot be altered through traditional file duplication methods of the operating system. Once the mining process is complete, mined blocks are then refined. Without being refined, the blocks are worthless. During the refining process, the blocks are compressed, encrypted, and digitally signed with a unique and secret key which is not made public. It is this secret key which is used to identify authenticity of $CMDC block sets.

Once blocks have been mined, refined, encrypted, digitally signed and verified, they are moved into a social distribution vault for storage and circulation. $CMDC is built on Gobble Chain private blockchain technology. In March 2022, Gobble Chain received its first major upgrade, version 2.0.

In April 2022, we introduced three new tokens (assets) - C3CT Token, POWER8 Token and TIDEIN Token. Token assets are now fully integrated into $CMDC and Gobble Chain. Token assets are mined and refined through a similar process to that of $CMDC blocks, but have multiple layers of encryption and use a different verification method to include a unique tokenID. Token assets are valued differently and are limited in supply.

Future upgrades of $CMDC will include integrated support for non-fungible tokens[?], or NFTs. The specification for NFT integration with Gobble Chain private blockchain technology is yet to be finalized and more information will be provided when available. When supported, NFT integration will provide the option for rights holders of digital images, audio, video and other digital artwork creations, to sell the rights to their original creations, as NFTs, which will be purchased using $CMDC.

FAQ
1. Is it a currency or an asset?
Both! $CMDC is the currency and tokens are the assets.
2. If I purchase, can I sell?
Yes! If you decide to sell, you will receive the exact value in return as your original purchase.
3. Will I lose money?
We do not consider money invested into purchasing $CMDC and token assets to be a loss.
4. Where does the money go?
All funds received from $CMDC and token asset purchases are directed into a dedicated holding account.
5. Is it right for me?
Only you can decide! We are developing a stable and secure platform for a digital currency and asset class. Like traditional stock market and cryptocurrency trading, the potential for financial loss is real and the liability for any loss(es) is the responsibility of the consumer.
